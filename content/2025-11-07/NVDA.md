•  Momentum cooling: a 10 % pullback from the recent high and a flattening MACD suggest traders are getting nervous into the Nov-19 earnings call. A miss could sting.  
•  Exaggerated expectations: aggressive $240 price targets risk a “priced-for-perfection” setup; one wobble and you can give back a lot of gains quickly.

Where the arguments land
The bear case is coherent but mostly rests on macro fears and the chance of an earnings disappointment—risks that are real but not new. The bull case is grounded in a powerful structural story (AI infrastructure) that is still early in its adoption curve. Yes, the bull managed to mangle last year’s revenue figure (it’s ±$27 B, not $130 B), but the bigger picture—rapid data-center growth, industry-leading margins, and a near-monopoly software stack—remains intact and more persuasive.

My call: Buy (with well-defined risk controls).

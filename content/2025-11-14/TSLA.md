My call: Sell / Underweight (align with the bear).

Bottom line

Tesla is still a remarkable company, but the stock has lost the margin of safety it once enjoyed. Until management proves it can defend profitability against a flood of new EV supply—and until governance stops making headlines for the wrong reasons—capital is better protected elsewhere.
